Rhinos

Rhinos are large herbivorous mammals, well known for their horned snouts. There are five different species of rhinos, and 11 subspecies of rhinos throughout the world.

    This five species includes:

  • White Rhino
  • Black Rhino
  • Sumatran Rhino
  • Greater one horned rhino
  • Javan Rhino
